# VCM-OS v0.6 Audit — 2026-05-11

## Executive Verdict
v0.6 is promising but not yet convincing as a general memory-system proof.

## What is convincing
- VCM can compile typed project memory into a compact context pack.
- VCM can match Full Context on the current 29-scenario synthetic dev benchmark.
- VCM beats Summary and RAG on that benchmark.
- Contamination and false-memory synthetic checks are clean.
- The rare-term code_change rescue fix was real, not just cosmetic.

## What is not convincing yet
- Holdout restore is too low (0.600).
- Adversarial hard restore is too low (0.333).
- Real codebase restore is too low (0.500).
- Report/version lineage is not clean enough.
- Sparse/exact-symbol retrieval is not strongly proven.
- The quality score may overweight keyword coverage.
- The evaluation may still be tuned to the pack builder.

## Judgment
- v0.5 Gold = credible synthetic milestone.
- v0.6 = incomplete generalization phase.
- v0.7 should be evaluation-integrity + project-state-restore release, not a feature-bloat release.
